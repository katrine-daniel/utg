üèõÔ∏è Historical Places in Delhi

Delhi's architectural grandeur and cultural legacy make it a must-visit for history lovers. Key landmarks include:

Red Fort

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and symbol of India's Mughal past.

Qutub Minar

A towering minaret showcasing Indo-Islamic craftsmanship.

Humayun's Tomb

A Mughal-era marvel that inspired the Taj Mahal.

India Gate

A war memorial that glows beautifully in the evening, honouring fallen soldiers.

üèØ Historical Places in Surat

Surat, known for its diamond and textile industries, also offers rich historical and cultural experiences. Must-visit sites include:

Dutch Garden

A colonial-era cemetery with European-style architecture and serene surroundings.

Sardar Patel Museum

A heritage museum showcasing Surat's history, art, and culture.

Surat Castle

Built in the 16th century to protect the city from Portuguese invasions.

Ambika Niketan Temple

A peaceful riverside temple dedicated to Goddess Ambika.
