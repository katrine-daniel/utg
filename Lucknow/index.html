Frequently Asked Questions

The distance between Delhi and Lucknow by car is approximately 550 kilometers via NH44 and NH30. The journey typically takes ~9-12 hours depending on traffic conditions, route taken, and stops made along the way.

Delhi is approximately 550 km from Lucknow by road. The most common routes include taking NH44 via Ghaziabad and then connecting to NH30. Alternative routes via NH9 are also available, though they may add some extra distance.

The most budget-friendly options are: Train for sleeper class, Bus for regular/AC buses, and Shared cab services . Booking in advance can help secure better deals.

Suggested stops for a memorable road trip:

  • Ghaziabad - First major stop, good for breakfast
  • Moradabad - Famous for brass handicrafts
  • Bareilly - Lunch break, try local cuisine
  • Shahjahanpur - Historical significance

Multiple daily flights operate between Delhi (DEL) and Lucknow (LKO). Major airlines include IndiGo, SpiceJet, Air India, and Vistara. Flight duration is approximately 1.5 hours
