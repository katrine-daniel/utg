City Highlights

Delhi - The Capital

Political heart of India with magnificent monuments

Red Fort

UNESCO World Heritage Site and symbol of India's Mughal heritage

Qutub Minar

A towering minaret showcasing Indo-Islamic architecture

India Gate

A war memorial that honours fallen soldiers and glows beautifully at night

Humayun's Tomb

A stunning Mughal-era tomb that inspired the Taj Mahal

Bhopal - Lake City

Nawabi elegance with natural beauty

Bharat Bhavan

A multi-arts complex showcasing tribal and contemporary art

Taj-ul-Masjid

One of the largest mosques in India, known for its grand architecture

Bhimbetka Caves

UNESCO World Heritage Site with prehistoric cave paintings and ancient rituals

Upper Lake

A scenic spot perfect for boating and sunset views

Explore Your Journey: Travel Experiences

Discover unique ways to travel and memorable experiences from Delhi to Bhopal

Flight Connect

Premium Air Travel

  • Journey Time: ~1.5-2 hours
  • Fast and convenient, ideal for business travellers
  • Multiple daily flights by IndiGo, Air India, Akasa Air

Rail Journey

Classic Train Journey

  • Journey Time: ~8-12 hours
  • Popular: Shatabdi Express & Bhopal Express
  • Budget-friendly and comfortable options

Road Adventure

Ultimate Road Trip Experience

  • Route: Delhi to Bhopal via NH46
  • Great for scenic drives and cultural stopovers
  • Smooth highways and picturesque countryside
